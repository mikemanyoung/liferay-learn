Start with why

Through the WHY, a story will arise, which will create trust and make an idea, a service, or a project unique. As Simon Sinek has said, having loyal users is all about attracting the people who share your fundamental beliefs. Remember: People don't buy what you do. They buy why you do it. I'll say it again: People don't buy what you do. They buy why you do it.
